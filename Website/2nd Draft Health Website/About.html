The Organisation and Our Mission

The Nelson's COPD Health Organisation is a non-profit organisation that supportingly wants to spread awareness about this chronic respiratory disease called Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease or COPD. Not only that this health organisation wants to prevent more cases and people getting affected by this disease, this organisation also aims to help and support the victims, or the people who has COPD. They provide ways, services, and information, anything that could help spread awareness and give aids to COPD victims that would make everyone have healthy lungs and breathe clean air.
