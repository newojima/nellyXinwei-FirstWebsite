What is COPD?

Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease or COPD in an overview is a chronic disease that obstructs the airways of our lungs, making it difficult to breathe air. There are further information about this medical condition regarding the causes of this disease, who is at risk, what are the symptoms, and how to treat and prevent this disease.
