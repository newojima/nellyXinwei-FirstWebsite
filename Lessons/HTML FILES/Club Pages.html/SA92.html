"Do-It-Yourself (DIY) Club"

"Do-It-Yourself Club : A Creative Club that helps people to be independent and skillful"
How to Join DIY Club


To join the club, you must apply at the Multi-Purpose-Center (MPC) in Xavier School San Juan for more information about the club and to apply for it.You may talk to the club moderators who are the representatives of the club.

Meeting Day
The Club meetings are usually on every Friday of each week.There is n specific day in the 7-Day Cycle for the meetings of this club.The club will only last for 1 hour every meeting.Usually ends on 4:00 pm every Friday but note that there might be some extensions for the activities that will be done in the club.
