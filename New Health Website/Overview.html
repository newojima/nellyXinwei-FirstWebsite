Overview

What is Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease? To begin with, let us first define the terms on the name of this disease or condition:

  • Chronic: A condition that is persistent in a long term, making it hard to eradicate.
  • Obstructive: Causing a blocking in effect, causing difficulties and delays in action or process.
  • Pulmonary: Relates or affects the lungs.
Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease or COPD in an overview is a chronic disease that obstructs the airways of our lungs, making it difficult to breathe air. There are further information about this medical condition regarding the causes of this disease, who is at risk, what are the symptoms, and how to treat and prevent this disease.
